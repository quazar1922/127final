Getting to Know the Creator and Editor of Single, Happy and Free!

Hello. My name is Vonetta Cartwright. I am the creator and editor of Single, Happy and Free magazine. For 50 years, I have had to justify not being married or having children. I have decided to live these last 50 years being true to myself. Life is meant to be lived according to the choices that you make. This magazine is a tribute to the women that decided to live according to their own truth. Join me on this journey to self discovery and fufillment.
