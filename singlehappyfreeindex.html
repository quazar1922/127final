How many times have you had to explain being childless by choice in a society that emphasizes motherhood as a main lifestyle for women? Single, Happy and Free is a magazine that celebrates being a woman who is unemcumbered by the historical and sterotypical views of womanhood. Exciting articles, amazing photos, and videos that show women who are single, free, and happy living the adventure called life. This magazine celebrates women that chose not to have children to follow their dreams.
